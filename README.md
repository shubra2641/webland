# 🚀 Webland Platform - The Complete SaaS Solution for Digital Commerce

**Webland Platform** is a powerful, all-in-one SaaS solution that lets you build a complete digital commerce business. Create stunning landing pages, manage products (physical/digital), handle subscriptions, integrate AI content generation, and accept payments - all from one platform.

---

## ✨ **Why Webland Platform?**

✅ **Multi-Vendor Ready** – Each user gets their own store  
✅ **AI-Powered Content** – Generate product descriptions & landing pages  
✅ **80+ Currencies** – Including all Arab currencies  
✅ **Multi-Language RTL** – Full Arabic support  
✅ **11+ Tracking Pixels** – Facebook, Google, TikTok, Snapchat...  
✅ **4 Payment Gateways** – Stripe, PayPal, Razorpay, Bank Transfer  
✅ **Wallet System** – Internal credits for PAYG plans  
✅ **Landing Page Builder** – Drag & drop + AI generation  
✅ **Subscription Management** – Complete billing system  
✅ **White Label Ready** – Sell to agencies

## 🔥 **Key Features**

### 🤖 **AI Integration**
- 5 AI Providers: Gemini, OpenAI, Claude, Groq, Perplexity
- Generate product SEO content
- Generate complete landing pages
- Token-based quota system

### 💳 **Payment System**
- Stripe, PayPal, Razorpay + Bank Transfer
- Wallet/credits system
- Webhook handling for all gateways
- Automatic subscription management

### 📊 **Tracking & Marketing**
- 11 Pixel services (Facebook, Google, TikTok, LinkedIn...)
- Pixel Manager for central control
- Server-side events (CAPI)

### 🌐 **Multi-Language & Currency**
- 80+ currencies supported
- Full RTL/LTR support

- Multi-language content

### 📦 **Product Management**
- Physical & digital products
- Inventory management
